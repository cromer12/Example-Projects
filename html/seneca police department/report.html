Report Crime
If you need immediate response, dial 864-885-2718. In case of an EMERGENCY, dial 9-1-1.

At Seneca Police department, your tips are valued and given the utmost attention. We are providing you with an avenue to provide information to the department to assist us in keeping the community safe.

While all crime tips are anonymous, there are times when we may need to contact you for a follow up or to advise you of the steps taken to properly address your tip, especially if you feel that your tips have not been addressed. Your identity will always be protected but please consider leaving an email address or phone number to contact you. All information is guarded and only given to the appropriate investigating officer.

Please complete the form below to leave your tip which will be forwarded to the necessary party.
