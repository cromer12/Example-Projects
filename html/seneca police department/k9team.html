K-9 Team
In 2011, donations from many local organizations, businesses, and private and professional citizens allowed the Seneca Police Department to purchase K-9 Ava . K-9 Rogue was purchased by the department in 2016 utilizing seized drug dealer assets and no tax dollars. German Shepherd, Ava, and Belgian Malinois, Rogue are passive alert K-9s trained in narcotics detection, protection, and tracking. Ava and Rogue work with the officers of Seneca Police Department and other agencies to provide a proactive response to drugs and crime within the City of Seneca and other local jurisdictions.
