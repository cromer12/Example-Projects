Job Announcements:

Police Officer

Minimum qualifications include a minimum age of 21, high school graduate or equivalent, excellent physical condition, good moral character, excellent communication skills, and able to withstand a rigid background investigation including polygraph, credit check, written exam, and psychological exam.
Persons certified as police officers through the South Carolina Criminal Justice Academy will receive special consideration. 
Applications will be received until the position is filled. 
Interested persons may apply at the Seneca Police Department or Seneca City Hall.

 AN EQUAL OPPORTUNITY/AFFIRMATIVE ACTION EMPLOYER
The City of Seneca does not discriminate on the basis of race, color, national origin, sex, religion, age, and disability status in employment or the provision of services.
